D.0 U.S.Lab

FHIR US Lab Implementation Guides (IG)

The US Laboratory Order Implementation Guides consists of three specifications:

  1. US Laboratory Order Implementation: laboratory orders from the ambulatory care setting to the laboratory
  2. US Laboratory Report Implementation: laboratory results from the laboratory to provider in ambulatory care setting
  3. US Laboratory Report to Public Health Implementation: laboratory results from the laboratory to the local public health jurisdiction.

Each IG contains the following sections:

  • Definitions, Interpretations and Requirements common to all USLab actors
  • Conformance Requirements for each USLab actor
  • USLab profiles
  • Operations
  • Value Sets
  • Concept Mappings
  • Examples

Published by: HL7 Orders and Observations Workgroup

Primary Author/Editor: Eric M Haas, Health eData Inc.
