Payer Extract Cohort Definitions for Payer Quality Measures

Introduction

This implementation guide contains definitions and resources to describe the cohorts and data requirements to calculate payer quality measures. The resources can be used to automate the extraction of data required to support the calculation of payer quality measures.

This guide focuses on cohort definitions to support the following six payer measures:

MeasureDescription
BCSBreast Cancer Screening
CBPControlling High Blood Pressure
CCSCervical Cancer Screening
CDCComprehensive Diabetes Care
COLColorectal Cancer Screening
ABAAdult BMI Assessment
